  • LLM-based Sentiment Analysis of Slovak Tweets

    This was my final semester project for my graduate-level Natural Language Processing class, LING 581. Since the first transformer-based Slovak language model of significant size, SlovakBERT , was published in October of 2021, I decided to use it to replicate their then state-of-the-art performance of sentiment classification in the Slovak language. This was my first experience reproducing the results of an academic paper, and I learned a lot as I used the Hugging Face and queried the Twitter API to collect the data I needed.
