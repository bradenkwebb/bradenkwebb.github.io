I recently graduated (April '23) from Brigham Young University with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and minors in Computer Science, Linguistics, and Logic.

In May 2023, I began my career as a Post-Bachelors Research Associate at Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, conducting research in the AI & Data Analytics division. My work there has spanned both the Statistical Modeling for Domain Science team and the ML Theory team.

I'm an avid learner, and I consume lots of books, podcasts, and spend a lot of time on Wikipedia. I think sites such as Goodreads or The StoryGraph are the highest form of social media. I'm broadly interested in deep learning (especially transformers), information geometry, theoretical computer science, linguistics (especially Slavic linguistics), computational musicology, and philosophy.

In my free time I enjoy tennis and pickleball, playing piano, and exploring Boston with my wife.
